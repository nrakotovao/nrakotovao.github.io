    Fampidirana


    Raha hampiditra ny package flutter_html ao @ app dia alefa ity kaomandy ity: 

    flutter pub add flutter_html
    Io kaomandy io dia manampy andalana iray flutter_html: ^3.0.0 ao @ fisie pubspec.yaml, section dependencies. Tonga dia miantso ihany koa ny kaomandy flutter pub get izay manavao ny lisitry ny package local.


    Isaky ny fisie Dart hampiasa ny package flutter_html amin'izay dia ialohavana ny fiantsoana: 

    import 'package:flutter_html/flutter_html.dart';


    FANAMARIHANA:
    Rehefa manao "flutter pub add flutter_html" dia ny version 2.2.1 no ampidiriny (hita ao @ pubspec.yaml) na dia ny version 3.0.0 ary no efa hita ao @ pub.dev. Torohevitra avy @ flutter ny fanandramana ny kaomandy "flutter pub outdated", saingy tsy mampiova inona fa mijanona ho io ny version farany azo ampiasaina hatreto.
